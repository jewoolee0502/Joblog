# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Job seekers actively applying to positions. They use Joblog during their job search to track applications, connect email accounts for automatic status updates, and maintain an organized pipeline. Context: stressed, juggling multiple applications, need clarity and control.

## Product Purpose

Joblog is an AI-powered job application tracker. It connects to Gmail/Outlook to automatically detect status changes, organizes applications in a Kanban pipeline, and surfaces nudges for follow-ups. Success looks like: users always know where every application stands without manual effort.

## Brand Personality

Clean, efficient, reliable. Professional without being corporate. Warm enough to feel approachable during a stressful process, but never frivolous. The tool should feel like a calm, competent assistant.

## Anti-references

- Corporate enterprise tools (Salesforce, SAP): overwhelming complexity, gray lifelessness, feature bloat
- Generic SaaS templates: purple gradients on white, stock photography, cookie-cutter layouts
- AI slop aesthetics: Inter font, predictable card grids, every element identical

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration**: every element communicates, nothing is purely ornamental
2. **Calm confidence**: the interface should reduce anxiety, not add to it; muted tones, steady rhythm
3. **Status at a glance**: the most important information (where do my applications stand?) is always visible and scannable
4. **Quiet intelligence**: AI features work in the background; the interface surfaces results, not process
5. **Respect the user's time**: fast to scan, fast to act, zero unnecessary clicks

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Sufficient color contrast for status indicators (not color-only differentiation)
